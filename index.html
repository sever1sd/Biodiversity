Belly Button Biodiversity Dashboard

This website is an exploration of the bacterial ecosystem present within belly buttons. Specifically, the data tracks the different types and total population of bacteria present in individual belly buttons. Additionally, it explores the impact of directly washing a belly button has on the types and populations of bacteria present.
